Problem of the Month (September 2015)

This month we pack unit squares into small polyominoes. The problems of packing squares into squares, dominoes, and L-triominoes have been studied before. This month we pack squares into straight triominoes and the non-square tetrominoes. What is the smallest of these polyominoes that contains n non-overlapping unit squares?
