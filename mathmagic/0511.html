Problem of the Month (May 2011)

Suppose f and g are integer functions, with f(n) ≤ n and g(n) ≥ n.
For small integers 0 ≤ n ≤ 100, we ask for the smallest number of iterations of f or g to return to n.
We also ask for the smallest number n requiring k operations to return to n.
Some pairs of functions are more interesting than others.
We restrict our attention to pairs which have relatively few cycles.
Can you extend one of the tables below, or find another interesting pair of functions f and g?
