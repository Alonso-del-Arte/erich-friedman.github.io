Roll-of-the-Dice Puzzles

Put a die with 1-6 pips in each square. The clues below and to the right indicate how that row or column could best be scored in Yahtzee. The hierarchy of hands is given below:

Y = Yahtzee: 5 identical dice
LS = Large Straight: 5 dice in numerical order
SS = Small Straight: 4 dice in numerical order
FH = Full House: 3 of one die, 2 of another
4Kn = 4 of a Kind: 4 identical dice, 1 non-matching die, with total n
3Kn = 3 of a Kind: 3 identical dice, 2 non-matching dice, with total n
