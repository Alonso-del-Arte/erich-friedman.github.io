Starry Night Puzzles

Put exactly one white circle (sun), one black circle (moon), and one star in every row and column of the grid. Symbols will not touch similar symbols diagonally. A sun or moon on the side of the grid indicates which is closer to the star in that row or column. A star on the side of the grid indicates that the sun and moon are the same distance to the star in that row or column.
