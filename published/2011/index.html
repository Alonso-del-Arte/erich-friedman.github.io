Figuring Out 2011 Puzzles

In each problem, use the 5 digits, along with the symbols + – × ÷ ( ) as necessary, to make an expression equal to 2011. No other symbols are allowed. For example, for 4, 5, 7, 7, 8, the answer would be 74 – 5 × 78 = 2011.
