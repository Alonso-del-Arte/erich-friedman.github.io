Pathfinder

Draw a path from Start (S) to Finish (F) that moves horizontally and vertically through the centers of the squares in each grid, and does not visit any square more than once. The numerical clues above and left of the grid indicate the total number of points in each row and column. A horizontal or vertical line through a square counts as 1 point, and a turn counts as 2 points. Ignore the Start and Finish squares when calculating points. The first puzzle is solved for you.
