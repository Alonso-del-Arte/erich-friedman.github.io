Starry Night Puzzles

Put exactly one white circle (sun), one black circle (moon), and one star in every row and column of the grid. Symbols will not touch similar symbols diagonally. A circle on the side of the grid indicates the color of the circle closer to the star in that row or column. A star on the side of the grid indicates that the circles in that row or column are the same distance to the star. Below is a solved puzzle as an example. Each puzzle below has a unique solution.
