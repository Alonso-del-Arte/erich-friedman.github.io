Holiday Puzzles 2011

1. RINGING IN THE NEW YEAR
 
Start at 2011. By moving through the maze and doing any arithmetic operations you encounter, exit the maze with a result of 2012. You may pass through an operation several times, but not twice in a row.

2011 2012

2. SYMMETRICAL GIFTS

Santa has laid out 3 types of presents on a workbench. Add some more gifts so that the gifts in each row and column form a palindromic sequence, reading the same forward as backward.

3. THE SANTA CLAUSES
Insert 1 or 2 letters per box to spell out the 9 different names below.

4. POLAR BEAR

Chop this polar bear into 5 equal pieces. The pieces can be rotated and reflected, but will be the same size and shape.

5. CHRISTMAS LIGHTS

Put a positive number in each light. Each number in the top row is a single digit. Each number not the top row is the sum of the numbers in the two lights just above it. The 2 red lights contain the same number, as do the 2 yellow lights, and the 2 green lights, and there are no other duplicate numbers.
