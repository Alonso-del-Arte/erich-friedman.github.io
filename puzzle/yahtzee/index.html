Yahtzee Puzzles

Put a die with 1-6 pips in each square. The clues below and to the right indicate how that row or column could best be scored in Yahtzee.

Y = Yahtzee, 5 identical dice
LS = Large Straight, 5 dice in numerical order
SS = Small Straight, 4 dice in numerical order
FH = Full House, 3 of one die, 2 of another
4Kn = 4 of a Kind with total n
3Kn = 3 of a Kind with total n
